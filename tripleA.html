Triple A Club at RCC

The Triple A club is a club that is made up of Animators, Artists and Authors, all dedicated to bringing their creative works to life. Through digital art, animation, script and comic writing, this group wishes to express its creative abilities to Rockland Community College, and even to other local schools, film festivals and organizations.
