Robotics Club at RCC

Want to build a robot? Thanks to ever-improving 21st century technology, this task is not science-fiction fantasy. Join the Robotics Club to ease your entry into our brave new world. Introduction to the concepts and practices of designing, building and programming robots. We adjust to student interests each semester, with multiple approaches and goals achieved using robot kits, robotics programming, and simulation software. Get started on your own mechanical or virtual creation by joining the Robotics Club!
