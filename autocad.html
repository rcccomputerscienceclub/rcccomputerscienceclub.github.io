AutoCAD Club

The AutoCAD Maker's club offers the ability to experience the design process to create and model objects. Drawing, drafting, modelling, 3D printing and prototyping is The mission/purpose of the CAD Maker’s Club to expose and bring forward CAD and related programs to familiarize students with the process of design and creation. Those already in the CAD field can explore this process to begin to 3D-printor otherwise produce prototype ideas and begin to better understand how this field related to real world disciplines. With CAD software and 3-D printers, we can expand upon the existing CAD courses.
